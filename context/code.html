The Experience & Events

timeline The Night Walk

11:30 PM

Assembly @ Neelangarai

Briefing on do's and don'ts. Get your red-light torches ready.

12:30 AM

The Patrol Begins

Walking in silence, scanning the high-tide line for tracks.

02:00 AM

Nesting & Collection

If a nest is found, volunteers help measure and relocate eggs safely.

05:00 AM

Arrival @ Besant Nagar

End of the walk at the hatchery. Relocation of eggs to protected pits.
